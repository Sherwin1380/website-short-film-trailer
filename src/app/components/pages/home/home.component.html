Synopsis

Anastasia is an Ukrainian immigrant girl in Poland due to the war in her country.
She works sometimes as a photo model to make some money to pay for her flat and send money to her family in Ukraine.
On her way to a photo session, she started to receive urgents calls from her mother. She didn't want to answer because she feels that something bad is going on.

Film Work

Francisco Bassignana is the Director of Krakovia Producciones. He was born in Santiago de Chile into an Italian family. He studied law. At the same time, he began an artistic career. After he completed his law studies, he decided to travel to Europe. Here, he developed most of his artistic skills and qualifications including theater studies in Spain and pantomime studies in France.
He currently works as a film Producer and this short film is his first film directed by him.
He has won many awards in cinema festivals as a Film Producer all around the world.

Krakovia Producciones

Producer Company

Krakovia Producciones, is a production company founded by Francisco Bassignana that deals with audio-visual works.
The main mission of Krakovia Producciones is to become a cultural bridge between Europe and South America.
