Synopsis

Anastasia is an Ukrainian girl immigrant in Poland during the war in her country.
She work sometimes like a photo model to have money for pay her flat and send money to her family in Ukraine.
In her way to a photo session she started to receive urgents telephone calls from her mother.She don't want to answer because she feels that something bad is going on.

Film Work

Francisco Bassignana is the Director of Krakovia Producciones.He was born in Santiago de Chile into an italian family.He studied law.At the same time, he began an artistic career .After his law studied he decides to travel to Europe.He developed most of his artistic skills and qualifications including theater studies in Spain and pantomime studies in France.
He currently works as a film Producer and this short film is his first film like director.
Like a film Producer he has won many awards in cinema festivals all around the world.
